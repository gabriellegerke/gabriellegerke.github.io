Gabrielle Gerke

I am a goal-oriented, organized, dependable, caring, personable, moral, and ethical individual. I am a natural communicator with skills honed through my nine years of 4-H and have also enjoyed success in the visual arts area. These experiences have led me to pursue a double major in journalism/public relations and graphic journalism at Grand View University. I have thoroughly enjoyed the opportunities that I’ve had thus far in my classes, as I am now a junior. I currently write and design for the Viewfinder, Grand View’s campus magazine and am a department editor this fall. I have also become the social media coordinator for Grand View’s Campus Ministry team, organizing a social media campaign and posting content for their organization. Outside of school I am involved with the Grand View Dance Team, enjoy spending time with my family and friends, reading, and watching movies. I also love playing with my goldendoodle, Cici, and am a devout Christian.
